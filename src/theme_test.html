Regenerate Skagit — Theme Lab

Rooted in Land, Water, and People

Regenerate Skagit invites us to listen, imagine, and belong within the bioregion of the Skagit watershed.

This page exists to test atmosphere. Not components. Not dashboards. Just tone, contrast, and whether the page invites you to stay.

“Place is not just where something happens. It is part of what happens.”

What we’re cultivating

  • A sense of place grounded in land and water
  • Human relationships that are reciprocal, not extractive
  • Stories that carry memory forward

Theme intent

Each theme reflects the same valley at different times and moods. Calm. Legible. Unrushed. If it feels like an app, the theme needs adjustment.

Links should be visible but not loud. This is a link. It should feel like part of the text, not a call to action.
