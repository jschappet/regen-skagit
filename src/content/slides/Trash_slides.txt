## The Tension We Live In
We want alignment with the earth.  

But try building a house without:  
- Synthetic glues  
- Portland cement  
- Non-renewable materials  

Modern systems are extractive by default.  

So the question becomes:  

*How do we build a culture that makes alignment possible?*

## Why Art?
Art changes how we relate.  

When we draw native plants,  
we remember where we are.  

When we imagine the river’s perspective,  
we think differently about the river.  

Turning toward nature.  
Turning toward each other.
