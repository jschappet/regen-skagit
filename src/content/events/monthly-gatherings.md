The Monthly Gatherings create a steady rhythm of connection and reflection.

Rather than one-off events, these gatherings are an ongoing practice of coming together to explore what matters most to us — our lives, our work, our relationships to land and water, and the future we are shaping together.

Each gathering is guided by simple prompts and light facilitation to help conversations remain grounded, respectful, and manageable. There is no expectation to speak. Listening is considered a full form of participation.

Continuity builds trust. Over time, shared understanding deepens and community becomes more visible.
