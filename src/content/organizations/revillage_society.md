## About ReVillage Society

ReVillage Society is a community organization dedicated to exploring **regenerative living** at a human scale. Their work includes creating cooperative housing models, hosting educational workshops, and stewarding land in ways that restore ecological function while strengthening social connections.

## Core Initiatives

- **Community Living:** Support for shared housing and cooperative governance models.
- **Local Food Systems:** Working with farms and local markets to build resilient, bioregional food networks.
- **Ecological Stewardship:** Land restoration projects, habitat improvement, and conservation practices.
- **Learning & Workshops:** Events, apprenticeships, and skill-sharing for members and neighbors.

## Vision

ReVillage Society envisions a network of communities where humans and the land thrive together, where learning, work, and social connection are deeply intertwined, and where regeneration happens at multiple scales — from soil to society.

## Get Involved

Membership, volunteering, and attending events are all welcome. ReVillage Society values hands-on participation as a way to deepen connection with the community and the bioregion.
