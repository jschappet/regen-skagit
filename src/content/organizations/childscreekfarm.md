## About Childs Creek Farm

Childs Creek Farm blends agricultural heritage with ecological stewardship. Over time, the family farm has been transformed into a vibrant **Conservation and Arts & Ecology site**, creating a space for education, ecological restoration, and community engagement.

## Core Initiatives

- **Habitat Restoration:** Bringing salmon back to local streams, planting native species, and restoring wetlands.
- **Biodiversity in Action:** Gardens, pastures, and waterways designed to support pollinators, birds, and other wildlife.
- **Arts & Education:** Hands-on learning opportunities, workshops, and events connecting people to the land and its stories.
- **Community Engagement:** Hosting visitors, volunteers, and educational programs for residents and regional learners.

## Vision

Childs Creek Farm envisions a regenerative future where **agriculture, ecology, and art** coexist, teaching humans to live in harmony with the land while actively restoring ecosystems.

## Get Involved

Visitors, volunteers, and students are welcome to participate in restoration projects, creative workshops, or simply experience the farm as a living classroom.
